A good portfolio does more than display work. It also shows how you think.

“Creating this portfolio artifact helped me move beyond memorizing algorithm names and toward understanding how model choice connects to data structure, problem framing, and real-world AI system design.”

The algorithm landscape project sharpened my ability to explain technical material clearly and visually. It also reinforced an important lesson: effective AI work is not only about knowing models, but about knowing when and why to use them.

Employers often want evidence that a candidate can connect theory to application. This portfolio is built to show that I can study concepts, structure them clearly, and present them in a way that supports practical implementation.

That mix of communication, engineering context, and AI reasoning is central to the kind of roles I’m pursuing.
